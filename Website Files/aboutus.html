About StudySesh



This website was created as part of my college EPQ. An EPQ or extended project qualification requires a student to make a project about a subject or topic that he likes. In my case, I decided to create a website, as my favorite subject is Computer science.


I started thinking of ideas for a website. I wanted to create something relatable to people and students like. I then decided that creating a website that could help those type of people could be really fun and fullfilling so I came up with the idea of making a website that had GCSE revision material, and proceeded to name it StudySesh.


The main purpose of StudySesh is to aid GCSE you in your revision by providing resources like past papers and subject aimed notes. The website will also help students find useful material for revision such as YouTube video tutorials or other websites that might help you, by offering you a section with numerous sources linking to this materials.
