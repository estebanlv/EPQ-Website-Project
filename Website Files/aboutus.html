About StudySesh




The main purpose of StudySesh is to aid GCSE students in their revision by providing resources like past papers and subject aimed notes. The website will also help students find useful material for revision such as YouTube video tutorials or other websites that might help them, by offering them a section with numerous sources linking to this materials.


Learn more

Need help revising for you GCSEs? Go to the materials page for some help!!

Meet the team

Esteban Lanza

Esteban Lanza is the owner and creator of StudySesh. He is a college student currently going to Peter Symonds. His idea to create StudySesh developed when he decided to take an Extended Project at college, and decided to take the idea and convert it into a reality.
